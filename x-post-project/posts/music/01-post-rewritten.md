# Post 3: New Album Release Strategy

## Original Content
Caroline Shaw and VC Artist Andrew Yee's New Album "Or, The Whale"

## Rewritten Content
Caroline Shaw and Andrew Yee just dropped "Or, The Whale" - and the release strategy is worth studying.

What they did right:
1. Teaser campaign: 3 months of behind-the-scenes content
2. Collaborative storytelling: Both artists shared their creative process
3. Limited edition vinyl: Created urgency and collectibility
4. Live streaming: Connected with fans worldwide

Marketing tactics:
- Spotify playlist placements
- Music blog exclusives
- Instagram Reels showing studio sessions
- Email newsletter for direct fan engagement

The result? #1 on classical charts, 500K streams in first week, sold-out vinyl pre-orders.

Independent artists: Take notes. This is how you compete with major labels.
